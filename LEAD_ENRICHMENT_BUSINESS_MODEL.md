## üîê Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

1. **API Provider Rate Limits**
   - Solution: Implement queue system with backoff
   - Buffer: Use 80% of API limits
   - Monitoring: Track rate limit headers

2. **Cost Overruns**
   - Solution: Hard caps per tier
   - Alerts: Usage warnings at 80%
   - Safety: Overage approval workflow

3. **Data Quality**
   - Solution: Validation pipelines
   - Fallbacks: Multiple data sources
   - Feedback: Report quality issues

### Business Risks

1. **Competitor Response**
   - Risk: Price wars
   - Mitigation: Focus on AI differentiation
   - Strategy: Build moat with data quality

2. **API Pricing Changes**
   - Risk: Provider increases prices
   - Mitigation: Multi-provider strategy
   - Buffer: 70%+ margins allow flexibility

3. **Customer Concentration**
   - Risk: Large customer churns
   - Mitigation: Diversify customer base
   - Safety: No customer > 20% revenue
