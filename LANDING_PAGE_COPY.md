## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: How is EverReach different from a traditional CRM?**
A: Traditional CRMs are built for sales teams tracking transactions. EverReach is built for humans maintaining relationships. We focus on who you know, not what you sell.

**Q: How does the Warmth Score work?**
A: Our proprietary algorithm uses exponential decay to track relationship health. Every interaction warms up a contact. Over time, without interaction, the score naturally decays. You can customize decay rates based on relationship importance.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR/CCPA. Your data is yours ‚Äî we never sell or share it with third parties.

**Q: Can I import contacts from other platforms?**
A: Yes! Import from Google Contacts, phone contacts, CSV files, or by uploading business cards. Our AI handles the rest.

**Q: How accurate is the AI message generation?**
A: Our AI analyzes your entire relationship history, writing style, and contact context to generate messages that sound authentically like you. Most users send AI-generated messages with minimal editing.
