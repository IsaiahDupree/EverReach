# EverReach 30-Day Organic Content Calendar

## Strategy Overview

**Goal**: Drive waitlist signups through organic social content  
**Primary CTA**: everreach.app/waitlist  
**Tone**: Direct, slightly provocative, empathetic to the pain  
**Voice**: Founder/expert who's been there

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of Content | Purpose |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| Pain Point Agitation | 40% | Make the problem feel urgent |
| Educational Value | 30% | Position as the expert |
| Social Proof / Results | 20% | Build trust |
| Behind-the-Scenes | 10% | Build connection |

---

## Platform-Specific Notes

### LinkedIn
- Best for: Professional networking pain, B2B relationships
- Format: Text posts (1300 chars), carousels (8-10 slides), polls
- Timing: Tue-Thu, 8-10am or 5-6pm
- Hashtags: #networking #relationships #sales #founders (max 3-5)

### Twitter/X
- Best for: Hot takes, threads, quick tips
- Format: Single tweets, threads (5-10 tweets), quote tweets
- Timing: 8am, 12pm, 5pm, 9pm
- Engagement: Reply to networking/sales influencers

### Instagram
- Best for: Visual storytelling, younger audience
- Format: Reels (15-30s), carousels, Stories
- Timing: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm
- Use: Trending audio, captions on reels

### TikTok
- Best for: Raw, authentic, problem-focused content
- Format: Talking head, POV videos, stitches
- Timing: 7-9am, 12-3pm, 7-9pm
- Hook: First 1 second is everything
