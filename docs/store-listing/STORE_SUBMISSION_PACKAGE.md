# Long Description (Apple + Google)

EverReach is your AI-enhanced personal CRM that helps you remember important people, reconnect at the right time, and grow strong professional and personal relationships.

- Capture notes fast (text or voice). Add tags and interests automatically.
- See a unified context view: last topics, shared interests, and relationship “warmth.”
- Set goals (networking, business, personal) for smarter replies and follow-ups.
- Search everything instantly with tags, interests, and keywords.
- Import contacts and keep them organized with minimal effort.
- Review analytics to ensure you’re investing in the right relationships.

Whether you’re a founder, operator, or creator—EverReach keeps you top-of-mind with the people who matter most.

## Key Features

- Voice notes and transcripts
- Context summary: interests, last discussed topics, recent interactions
- Goal-based composition (networking, business, personal)
- Warmth score and relationship health
- Global search, tags, and interests
- Import/export contacts
- Unified message history and basic analytics
- Privacy-first design: your data is yours

## Who it’s for

- Professionals building strong networks
- Founders and operators who stay in touch deliberately
- Creators nurturing communities and collaborations

# Privacy & Data Safety

- **Data collected**
  - Personal info: email, display name, phone (optional) (account setup)
  - Contacts: imported contacts (optional, permission-based)
  - App activity: usage events (analytics)
  - Diagnostics: crash reports and performance metrics
  - Coarse location: city, state, zip (from profile, for ad attribution)
  - Device identifiers: fbp (Facebook app ID), client IP
- **Data handling**
  - Data is encrypted in transit
  - You can request account deletion via in-app support
  - No data is sold to third parties
  - Used to provide app features, analytics, ad measurement, and support
- **Tracking / ATT**
  - Meta Conversions API used for ad attribution (hashed PII only, with user consent)
  - ATT prompt is shown on iOS; tracking is gated on user permission
  - If user denies tracking, only anonymous aggregate events are sent
- **Third-party SDKs that collect data**
  - PostHog: product analytics (pseudonymous)
  - RevenueCat: subscription management
  - Meta Conversions API: ad measurement and attribution (with ATT consent)
